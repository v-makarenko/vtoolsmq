The leaderboard shows who ran the most non-NTC/non-stealth wells over the course of a week. Plates and wells are credited to individuals if they either named a plate according to the QTools plate naming standard, or if the plate was manually credited to an operator via the QTools plate metadata editor. Right now, a plate can only have a single owner, but this seems to be the most common case at this time. Unassigned plates may be credited below.
